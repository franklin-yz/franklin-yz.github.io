This project investigates how medication-taking behavior influences blood pressure and related outcomes in routine care.

Key components:

- Causal inference with longitudinal real-world data.
- Machine learning models for individualized treatment effect estimation.
- Integration of dispensing, prescription, and clinical outcome signals.

The long-term goal is to support personalized medication decisions in hypertension management.
