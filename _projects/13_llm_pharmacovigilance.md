This project builds NLP and deep learning pipelines for adverse drug event mining and medication safety surveillance.

Key components:

- LLM-assisted extraction and normalization of adverse event mentions.
- Integration with terminology systems such as RxNorm.
- Benchmarking against rule-based and ontology-based approaches.

Representative outputs include workshop publications in SMM4H/ACL and conference work in healthcare informatics.
