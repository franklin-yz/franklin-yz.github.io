This project studies how AI uncertainty communication affects pharmacist trust, reaction time, and decision quality.

Key components:

- Human-centered AI design and mixed-methods evaluation.
- Randomized and crossover experimental designs.
- Signal-detection-theory based performance analysis for pharmacist-AI teams.

The objective is safer and more reliable medication verification systems in practice.
