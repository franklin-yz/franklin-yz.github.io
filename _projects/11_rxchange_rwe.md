This work evaluates the clinical and economic impact of pharmacist-initiated RxChange communication with prescribers.

Key components:

- Retrospective analyses of large-scale electronic prescription workflows.
- Outcome and cost modeling for intervention effectiveness.
- Translation of findings into pharmacy workflow and policy insights.

This line of work is connected to first-author publications in JAPhA.
