I am a PharmD and PhD candidate at the University of Michigan working in real-world evidence (RWE), pharmacoepidemiology, and health outcomes research. My work generates decision-relevant evidence on medication use, drug safety, and treatment outcomes by applying epidemiologic study design, causal inference, and quantitative modeling to large electronic health record, pharmacy claims, and clinical-trial data.

My training spans [health services research](https://pharmacy.umich.edu/pharmacy-programs/phd/clinical-pharmacy-translation-science/health-services-research-track/) at the [College of Pharmacy](https://pharmacy.umich.edu), [scientific computing](https://micde.umich.edu/academic-programs/phd-in-scientific-computing/) at the [College of Engineering](https://engineering.umich.edu/), and [bioinformatics](https://medschool.umich.edu/departments/computational-medicine-bioinformatics/education/dual-degree-program) at the [Medical School](https://medschool.umich.edu/) at the University of Michigan. This cross-disciplinary foundation lets me work at the interface of observational study design, comparative effectiveness research, and translational data science.

I conduct research in the [Medication Data Science (MeDS) Lab](https://pharmacy.umich.edu/lab/medication-data-science-lab/) under the mentorship of [Dr. Corey Lester](https://pharmacy.umich.edu/people/lester-corey/), focusing on longitudinal modeling of real-world treatment patterns, medication adherence and exposure measurement, and medication safety. My recent first-author study in the [Journal of Managed Care & Specialty Pharmacy](https://www.jmcp.org/doi/10.18553/jmcp.2026.32.4.434) examines how written, filled, and sold prescription data influence real-world medication adherence and exposure measures. I presented related work at the [ISPOR](https://www.ispor.org/) 2026 Annual Meeting, where it received a Top 5% Research Presentation Award.

I currently work part-time in real-world evidence at [AbbVie](https://www.abbvie.com/) and recently completed a cooperative education (co-op) placement in quantitative pharmacology and model-based meta-analysis at [Merck](https://www.merck.com/). I earned my PharmD from the University of Michigan and my Bachelor of Science in Pharmaceutical Sciences from [China Pharmaceutical University](https://www.cpu.edu.cn/).
